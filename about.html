About VioletVixen

Violet lives in the PNW with her handsome and supportive husband and her service dog.
Violet started this pen name as a secret project so she could write spicy books without anyone finding out who she was.
After an extended hiatus, Violet is back and ready to share her stories with the world.
She has a passion for writing and creating characters that readers can connect with.
Her books are filled with steamy romance, complex characters, and engaging plots that will keep you hooked until the very end.
Violet is is a creative and imaginative writer who loves to explore different genres and themes in her work.
She is dedicated to providing her readers with high-quality stories that are both entertaining and thought-provoking.
Violet is excited to share her writing journey with her readers and hopes to inspire others to pursue their own creative passions.
